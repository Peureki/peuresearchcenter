How to:
Follow the recommended priority of events. This was a very experimental farm. At a time that Destroyer Lodestones were extremely spiking in price, I came up with this. This is essentially Draconis Mons meets Ember Bay. These are the only two maps that have a large quantity of events with Destroyers. I did a round of the meta in Draconis Mons, then went to Ember Bay to do the Karka escort, and the Molten Dominator event. Afterwards, we head back to Draconis Mons to do another meta.

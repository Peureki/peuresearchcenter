How to:
Follow the recommended priority of events. This farm is only during the week 4 of the map bonus rotation. Essentially this farm was about tagging events that spawns either a lot of mobs or champions. Both temple and Arah metas were included, but it is also hard to predict when they will spawn. I believe they have about a 2 hour cooldown, but since this farm was recorded 1.5 hours, it's safe to say that there's a chance that 1 or 2 of the meta events may not show up.

Interestingly enough, one of the hardest parts about this farm was that DPS is generally very high compared to what this farm used to be in 2012-2014. With that said, mobs die very very quickly so it's best to notify the squad to bring loot sticks rather than their raid builds.

How can you help:

Patreon: These funds will cover the server/hosting and the time it takes for me to create content for everyone. Any kind of donation will be appreciative!

Data collection: Most of the data has been collected by me. Some sources are from my guildmates or other reliable resources such as YouTube, Overflow, and other data collectors. I can't do everything though because it takes too long and there's so many different things. I'm looking specifically for:
- champion bags
- containers
- unidentified gear salvaging
- mystic forge conversions, and more.
Please DM me on Discord at #Peureki5452 so we can discuss how we can collaborate! The more data we get, the more accurate numbers we can provide for the average choya.

Join my open world trains! I post my trains on the front page and in the Overflow Discord. The more peeps, the better I can do the trains! Come chill and have fun in chat with me.

Route:
Start at Red Leaf Retreat Waypoint [&BLAJAAA=]. The whole green area of the zone is filled with Elder Wood and Verdant Herbs. Avoid most of the water as there isn't that many nodes there. I generally gathered everything for the benchmark, but it's good to focus on Elder Wood and Herb nodes as they will generate the most profits. I also avoided nodes that were swarmed with enemies. White Mantle are just not worth dealing with sometimes LOL.

I did this on 7 characters for the hour benchmark.

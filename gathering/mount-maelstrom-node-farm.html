Route:
From the left side of the map, start at Criterion Waypoint [&BMkCAAA=]. There will always be a lot of nodes around the waypoint and there will be a Rich Platinum Ore to the west.
,br> From the right side of the map, start at Old Sledge Site Waypoint [&BNQCAAA=]. Head north beyond an Asuran platform that there may be a possible Rich Platinum Ore. If not, head west towards the water and there will be a guaranteed Rich Platinum Ore. From there, head more northwest and there will be a trail of herbs, ores, and lots of trees.

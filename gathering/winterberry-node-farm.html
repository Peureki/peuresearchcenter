Route:
Start at Sorrow's Eclipse Waypoint [&BH0JAAA=]. A variety of mounts is recommended here because there will be a lot of different elevations of travel (thermal tubes, hills, etc). When going into a thermal tube, make sure to glide then mount when at the peak of your arc. If you take the thermal all the way, you may possibly be misplaced or be taken into another thermal to another location.

Bring condition cleanses for Chill. Bitterfrost mobs are cruel like that.

There's a node on the northeast sector of the map. I think that node is very out of the way and you're time may be better spent gathering the others.
